# Ancient Boons Reference Guide

> Ancient boons are by far the biggest amount of power you get from a selection screen in the entire game. Making the right choice here can snowball your entire run.

**Critical Rule:** Always look at your MAP before choosing a boon. Early shop? Scary path? Safe path? Your choice should depend on the map.

---

## Act 1 Boons (Neow)

### S-Tier (Almost Always Pick)

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Leafy Pus** | Transform one Strike and one Defend. Lose 10 max HP. | BEST BONUS. 10 max HP loss is nothing. Transforms = 2 removes + 2 random cards. Click almost every time. |
| **Lost Coffer** | Gain one card reward + one random potion | Best if scared of dying. Card reward snowballs. Potion prevents bricks. Always safe. Bottom of S. |
| **Small Capsule** | Obtain a random relic | Commons are insane in STS2. Immediate power + direction. Safe click. |

### A-Tier (Usually Pick)

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Cursed Pearl** | Gain 333 gold. Add Greed (unremovable curse) | Only with early shop! Buy relic + remove + card. Think of it as 250 gold + Strike→Greed trade. |
| **Golden Pearl** | Gain 150 gold | Solid if early shop. Less gold than Cursed Pearl but no curse. Flexible. |
| **Lead Paper Weight** | Choose one of two colorless cards | Colorless cards are redesigned to be generically good. Can get damage, energy gen, etc. |
| **New Leaf** | Transform one card | One remove + one random card. Solid direction. Transform Strike usually. |
| **Precarious Shears** | Remove 2 cards. Take 13 damage. | Map dependent! Don't pick on scary maps. Silent/Necro love this. Ironclad less so. |
| **Silver Crucible** | First 3 card rewards are upgraded. First chest is empty. | High value maps = farm with upgraded cards. Trade relic for early power. Don't skip first 3 cards! |

### B-Tier (Situational)

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Arcane Scroll** | Obtain a random rare card | Character dependent. Good on Necro (all rares good), risky on Ironclad (many duds). B/C tier. |
| **Nutritious Oyster** | Raise max HP by 11 | Makes rests stronger. Damage sponge. Take over Fury. |
| **Palmander** | Upgrade a card | Character dependent. Silent upgrades Neutralize (good). Others are mid. Saves some HP. |
| **Precise Scissors** | Remove one card | Just positive. Difference between 1 and 2 removes is BIG. Can combo with shop remove. |

### C-Tier (Rarely Pick)

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Booming Conch** | Draw 2 extra cards at start of elite combats | Does nothing in hallways or bosses. Only elites. Commits you to farming elites. |
| **Lava Rock** | Act 1 boss drops 2 relics | Does NOTHING until boss. Act 1 is hardest part. Skipping Neow bonus is bad. |
| **Torment** | Add One's Fury to your deck | Card is just a mediocre damage common. Not worth boss reward slot. |

### D-Tier (Almost Never Pick)

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Large Capsule** | Obtain 2 random relics. Add Strike + Defend to deck. | Extra basics HURT. Can brick you. No P-Box to remove basics. Regret every time. |
| **Scroll Boxes** | Lose all gold. Choose 1 of 2 card packs to add. | Losing gold hurts. Card packs often mediocre. Desperation only. |
| **Stony Humidifier** | Resting raises max HP by 5 | Delayed value. Pigeonholes you into resting. Does nothing if you upgrade. |

## Act 2 Boons (Orobas)

### S-Tier

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Pale's Blood** | Draw 1 extra card per turn | Machine Learning for free from turn 1. Lines up all combos. DISGUSTING. |
| **Bill's Claw** | Enchant all Defends with Goopy (block +1 per play, exhaust) | Removes all Defends from deck + makes them Genetic Algorithms. Go infinite enabler. #1 relic. |
| **Pale's Eye** | End turn without playing cards = exhaust hand + extra turn | Vault in a bottle. Brick protection + exhaust + infinite enabler. Never lost with it. |
| **Story Book** | Add Brightest Flame (0 cost, draw 2, gain 2 energy, lose 1 max HP) | Max HP loss doesn't matter. Card wins fights. Goes infinite easily. Never lost with it. |
| **Pandora's Box** | Transform all Strikes and Defends | Best relic if you have basics. Removes all bad cards. S-tier in Act 2, worse in Act 3. |

### A-Tier

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Alchemical Coffer** | Gain 4 potion slots filled with random potions | Bailout relic. Hoard potions for boss gauntlet. Solve problem fights. |
| **Driftwood** | Reroll each card reward once | Double your chances to find key cards. Snowballs quickly. |
| **Glass Eye** | Obtain 2 common packs, 2 uncommon packs, 1 rare pack | Fix your deck immediately. Find infinite pieces. Extra rare is huge. |
| **Radiant Pearl** | Start combat with Luminescence (retain, 0 cost, 2 energy) | Upgraded Miracle. Hold for big power turn. Great with Bellows relic. |
| **Pale's Wing** | Sacrifice card rewards to Pale. Every 2 sacrifices = 1 relic | You're already skipping cards. Free relics for doing what you do. |
| **Yummy Cookie** | Upgrade 4 cards | Strong in Act 2. Upgrades are hard to get. Small deck loops love upgrades. |

### B-Tier

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Touch of Orobas** | Replace starter relic with ancient version | Depends on character. Necro's Unbound Factory is best. Most others are C-tier. |
| **Pale's Tears** | Unspent energy = +2 energy next turn | Better than it reads. Can float energy consistently. Needs deck that can float. |
| **Golden Compass** | Replace Act 2 map with special safe path | Easy mode for Act 2. Two extra floors. Two chests. Elites at end. Gets worse when Act 4 added. |

### C-Tier

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Archaic Tooth** | Transform starter card into ancient version | Ranges from S to D depending on character/card. Hard to evaluate generally. |
| **Electric Shrimp** | Enchant a Skill with Imbued (auto-play turn 1) | Only Skills. Need haymaker skill. Can be broken with End of Days. Usually narrow. |
| **Sand Castle** | Upgrade 6 random cards | Mostly hits basics. Ceiling high but usually mid. |
| **Pumpkin Candle** | +1 energy per turn. Extinguishes at Act 3 start. | Short-term benefit only. Use to farm Act 2, but you need to win Act 3 without it. |
| **Pale's Growth** | Enchant card with Clone (duplicate at rest sites) | Wait for campfire + need clone-worthy card. Exponential but conditional. |
| **Pale's Horn** | Add 2 Relax cards (3 energy, 15 block, +2 cards/energy next turn) | 15 block not enough for skipping turn. Good with Pocket Watch. |
| **Pale's Legion** | Double block gain, sleeps 2 turns | Can't choose when it activates. Not enough output vs other options. |
| **See Glass** | See 15 cards from random other character. Choose any to add. | Other class cards usually bad. Maybe Necro is okay. |

### D-Tier

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Prismatic Gem** | +1 energy. Card rewards contain other colors. | Breaks card rewards. Can't find synergy pieces. Fun but bad. |
| **Big Hug** | Remove 4 cards. Add Soot when shuffle. | Competing effects. Want small deck = hate Soot. Weird design. |
| **Nutritious Soup** | Enchant all Strikes with 0 cost, unremovable | Stuck with Strikes forever. Just Slice that can't be removed. |
| **Seal of Gold** | Spend 5 gold per turn for +1 energy | Permanently poor. Can't afford removes. Weird. |
| **Toasty Mittens** | Exhaust top card, gain 1 Strength | Random exhaust = exhaust your best card. Curse. |
| **Toy Box** | Obtain 4 wax relics. One melts away each combat. | Relics gone by Act 3. Short-term only. |
| **Pale's Flesh** | +1 energy starting turn 3 | Waiting 2 turns for energy is bad. Want frontload. |

## Act 3 Boons (Nopet)

### S-Tier

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Runic Pyramid** | Don't discard hand at end of turn | BEST RELIC IN GAME. Play all combos together. Zero-cost stuff insane. Energy gen insane. SLAM IT. |
| **Beautiful Bracelet** | Choose 3 cards, enchant with Swift 3 (first play draws 3) | Draws 9 extra cards. Unbricks everything. Never felt bad. |
| **Diamond Diadem** | Play ≤2 cards = take half damage | Brick protection. Setup for free. Blocks for full often. Never lost with it. |
| **Distinguished Cape** | Lose 9 max HP. Add 3 Apparitions (Intangible) | Apps for 9 HP?! No downside. 3 turns to set up for free. Can loop intangible. |
| **Preserved Fog** | Remove 5 cards. Add unplayable innate eternal curse. | FOUR REMOVES for one bad card turn 1. Goes infinite immediately. |
| **Meat Cleaver** | Cook at rest sites: remove 2 cards, gain 9 max HP | Remove 6+ cards in act. Rest + remove combo. Never lost with it. |

### A-Tier

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Blessed Antler** | +1 energy. Shuffle 3 Daze into draw pile each combat | Daze is manageable. Energy helps setup. Good for boss gauntlet. |
| **Brilliant Scarf** | 5th card played each turn is free | Energy relic if you play 5+ cards. Many decks can. |
| **Iron Club** | Every 4 cards played, draw 1 | Can set up between combats. Higher ceiling than flat +1 draw. |
| **Sigh** | Gain 7 block at start of turn | Passive block forever. Helps setup. Good but boring. |
| **Spike Gauntlets** | +1 energy. Powers cost +1. | If you only have few powers, basically free energy. |
| **Signet Ring** | Gain 999 gold | Path to shops. Buy relics/removes. Crazy value. |
| **Jewelry Box** | Add Apotheosis (innate, upgrade all cards, costs 2→1) | Upgraded Apo is great. Helps bad runs. |
| **Ash Abe** | Transform 3 cards, upgrade them | Removes + upgraded randoms. Solid in both acts. |
| **Choices Paradox** | Start of combat: choose 1 of 5 random cards. Add Retain. | Anti-brick. Pick what you need for the fight. Better than it reads. |

### B-Tier

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Looming Fruit** | Raise max HP by 31 | Big HP = tank setup turns. Good if deck already wins. |
| **Glitter** | Enchant all card rewards with Glam (replay once) | By Act 3, you skip most cards. Bails out bad runs. |
| **Jeweled Mask** | Start combat: random power from draw pile to hand. Free. | Gets you set up faster. Solid if power-dependent. |
| **Empty Cage** | Remove 2 cards | Removes always good. Things do it better. |
| **Very Hot Cocoa** | +4 energy turn 1 only | Ice Cream / Innate powers make this broken. Situational otherwise. |
| **Whispering Earring** | +1 energy. Vaku plays turn 1 for you. | Less control but energy for bosses. Better than it looks. |

### C-Tier

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Calling Bell** | Gain unique curse + 3 relics | Curse hurts small decks. Better early game. |
| **Tri-Boomerang** | Enchant 3 attacks: cost -1 | Ironclad only basically. Pommel infinite. Others don't care. |
| **Fiddle** | Draw +2 per turn. Cannot draw during turn. | Necro/Defect only. Shuts down all draw cards. Value pile. |
| **Music Box** | Create ethereal copy of first attack each turn | Few decks rely on attack copying. Low ceiling. |
| **Saratalon** | Add 2 random curses + 3 Wishes (seek) | Curses add variance. Small decks don't want this. |
| **Tang's Whistle** | Add Whistle (3 energy, 33 damage, stun) | 3 energy is hard. Fun to bonk boss. Situational. |
| **Tusk** | First card each combat plays twice | Inconsistent. Need Innate. High roll dependent. |
| **Warhammer** | Kill elite = upgrade 4 random cards | Not fighting elites in Act 3. Useless. |

### D-Tier

| Boon | Effect | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Fur Coat** | Mark 7 random combats. Enemies have 1 HP. | By Act 3, deck should fight anything. Time save only. |
| **Claws** | Transform up to 6 cards into Maul (scaling attack) | Don't have 6 basics left. Slow scaling attacks meh. |
| **Crossbow** | Start turn: add random attack to hand, costs 0 this turn | Random crap in deck. Clogs small decks. Curse. |
| **Lord's Parasol** | Obtain everything from next merchant | Add all the garbage cards? Why? |
| **Blood Sucker Rose** | Add Enthralled (2 cost, must play first, eternal) + 1 energy | Permanent curse. Can brick important turns. |

## Character-Specific Notes

### Ironclad
- Leafy Pus is always great
- Precarious Shears less good (doesn't save as much HP in hallways)
- Corruption from Dusty Dew much worse than STS1 (no Feel No Pain)

### Silent  
- Precarious Shears is AMAZING (Survivor/Neutralize more often = less chip)
- Wraithform from Dusty Dew is S-tier
- Leafy Pus great

### Defect
- Bias Cognition worse than STS1 (no Artifact to negate)
- Infused Core (3 Lightning) is bad (random targeting, clogs orb slots)
- Leafy Pus great

### Necrobinder
- Precarious Shears DISGUSTING (blocks more = more Osty HP = more damage)
- Factory Unbound (summon 5 + 2/turn) is best character relic
- Forbidding Grimoire is broken (free removes = infinite)

### Regent
- Sealed Throne (gain star per card) enables star loops
- Precarious Shears good
- Leafy Pus great

## Quick Decision Guide

**Scared of dying? (Low HP, scary map):**
→ Lost Coffer (card + potion)
→ Small Capsule (random relic)
→ Nutritious Oyster (max HP)

**Safe map, want power:**
→ Leafy Pus (transform 2)
→ Precarious Shears (remove 2, take 13)
→ Silver Crucible (upgraded rewards)

**Early shop available:**
→ Cursed Pearl (333 gold)
→ Golden Pearl (150 gold)

**No good options:**
→ Arcane Scroll (random rare)
→ New Leaf (transform 1)

**Act 2/3 - Need to go infinite:**
→ Bill's Claw (Defends exhaust)
→ Pale's Eye (extra turn)
→ Meat Cleaver (remove at rest)
→ Preserved Fog (remove 5)

**Act 3 - Just win:**
→ Runic Pyramid
→ Distinguished Cape (Apparitions)
→ Diamond Diadem (half damage)
