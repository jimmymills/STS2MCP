# Slay the Spire 2 Boss Guide

## Overview

Check the boss icon at the top of the map at the start of each act! Build your deck knowing what you'll face.

---

# ACT 1 BOSSES

## Overgrowth Bosses (Act 1a)

### Vantom 🐍
**HP:** ~200 | **Difficulty:** Easy

**Mechanics:**
- 4-turn attack cycle, gaining Strength at end of each cycle
- Adds 3 Wound cards (unplayable) on turn 3
- Watch the tail - grows larger through the sequence

**Attack Pattern:**
1. Attack
2. Attack x2
3. BIG attack + inflicts 3 Wounds
4. Buffs self with Strength
5. *Repeats*

**Strategy:**
- Burn boss down before Wounds clog your deck
- Exhaust effects counter Wounds
- DPS race - kill fast before strength stacks

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Exhaust builds shine (Second Wind, Burning Pact eat Wounds)
- ✅ High damage early ends this fast
- ⚠️ Long fights = deck full of Wounds

---

### Ceremonial Beast 🦬
**HP:** ~220 | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Mechanics:**
- **Two phases** - behavior changes after hitting HP threshold
- **Plow buff** - stun boss by reducing HP below threshold
- Phase 1: Attacks + gains Strength each turn
- Phase 2: Applies Ringing debuff (card cost increase?)

**Phase 1 Pattern:**
1. Applies Plow buff to self
2. Attack + Empower (+Str)
3. *Repeats until HP threshold*

**Phase 2 Pattern (after Plow triggers):**
1. Stunned (FREE DAMAGE TURN!)
2. Strategic (Ringing debuff)
3. Attack
4. Attack + Empower (+Str)
5. *Repeats from step 2*

**Strategy:**
- Maximize damage on the stun turn!
- If you have lots of block but low damage, wait a turn before triggering Plow
- Block hard on Ringing turns

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Save big attacks for the stun turn
- ✅ Bloodletting + attacks dump tons of damage on stun
- ⚠️ Ringing every 3rd turn in Phase 2 is brutal

---

### Kin Shaman 👺
**HP:** ~180 (boss) + 2 minions | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Mechanics:**
- Boss + 2 minions - must deal with 3 enemies
- Minions have offset attack patterns (always some incoming damage)
- Can kill boss directly to end fight OR clear minions first

**Minion Pattern (offset):**
- Left: Empower → Attack → Attack x2 → *repeat*
- Right: Attack → Attack x2 → Empower → *repeat*

**Kin Shaman Pattern:**
1. Attack + Strategic (1 Frail)
2. Attack + Strategic (1 Weak)
3. Attack x3
4. Empower (+Str)
5. *Repeats*

**Strategy:**
- AoE clears minions fast → then focus boss
- OR high single-target burns boss, ignore minions
- Boss has slow 4-turn ramp - not too scary alone

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ AoE cards shine (Whirlwind, Cleave, Immolate)
- ✅ Stomp reduces cost with attacks played
- ⚠️ Frail + Weak debuffs stack if fight drags

## Underdock Bosses (Act 1b)

### Lagavulin Matriarch 😴
**HP:** ~230 | **Difficulty:** HARD

**Mechanics:**
- **Asleep for 3 turns** (or until you deal HP damage)
- Has Plating (takes reduced damage)
- Once awake: attacks + debuffs your Str AND Dex every 4th turn

**Attack Pattern (after waking):**
1. Attack
2. Attack x2 (scales with Str!)
3. Attack + Block
4. Strategic/Empower (-Str/-Dex to you, +Str to self)
5. *Repeats*

**Strategy:**
- **USE THE 3 SLEEP TURNS TO SET UP!**
- Play Powers, cycle deck, get your engine online
- Kill fast - the -Str/-Dex debuffs stack and cripple you
- Multi-hit attacks get wrecked by -Str debuff

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Setup Vicious+ while sleeping, then Bash for huge draw
- ✅ Play Bloodletting (Steady) after setup, pop off when ready
- ✅ Ornamental Fan, Poison, Doom bypass Str debuff
- ⚠️ DO NOT attack until you're ready
- ⚠️ Long fights = you deal 0 damage and can't block

---

### Waterfall Giant 💧
**HP:** ~250 | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Mechanics:**
- **Steam Eruption** - buffs up each turn, EXPLODES when boss dies
- Explosion damage = accumulated Steam Eruption stacks
- Boss does NOT gain Strength - attacks don't scale

**Attack Pattern:**
1. Applies Steam Eruption
2. Attack + Empower (Steam) + Strategic (Weak)
3. Small Attack + Empower (Steam)
4. Heal + Empower (Steam)
5. Big Attack + Empower (Steam)
6. Attack + Empower (Steam)
7. *Repeats from step 2*

**Strategy:**
- Kill FAST to minimize explosion damage
- Save block/Weak for the death explosion
- Boss is stunned + invulnerable before explosion - apply Weak then!

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Burst damage minimizes Steam stacks
- ✅ Save Intimidate/Uppercut for explosion turn
- ⚠️ Don't drag the fight - explosion gets huge

---

### Soul Fysh 🐟
**HP:** ~200 | **Difficulty:** Hard

**Mechanics:**
- **Beckon cards** - lose HP if they stay in hand at end of turn (bypasses block!)
- Gets Intangible every 4th turn (all damage reduced to 1)
- Applies Vulnerability

**Attack Pattern:**
1. Strategic (adds 2 Beckon to deck)
2. Attack
3. Attack + Strategic (adds 1 Beckon)
4. Empower (Intangible)
5. Attack + Strategic (Vulnerability)
6. *Repeats*

**Strategy:**
- Play or exhaust Beckon cards immediately
- Don't dump all damage into Intangible turn
- Kill before deck is flooded with Beckon

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Exhaust handles Beckon perfectly
- ✅ Burning Pact, Second Wind eat Beckon
- ⚠️ Intangible turn - set up, don't attack
- ⚠️ HP loss bypasses block!

# ACT 2 BOSSES (The Hive)

### The Insatiable 🪱
**HP:** ~350 | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Mechanics:**
- **Sandpit counter** starts at 4, ticks down each turn
- When Sandpit = 0, **instant death**
- Play Frantic Escape cards to increase counter by 1
- Frantic Escape costs increase over time

**Attack Pattern:**
1. Empower (Sandpit) + adds 6 Frantic Escape to deck
2. Attack x2
3. Big Attack
4. Empower (+Str)
5. *Repeats from step 2*

**Strategy:**
- Balance damage with playing Frantic Escape
- Don't hoard Frantic Escape - costs ramp up!
- Straightforward boss - just don't get eaten

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Frantic Escape is a Status - careful with status interactions
- ✅ High DPS ends this before costs get crazy
- ⚠️ Must play Frantic Escape every few turns

---

### Knowledge Demon 📚
**HP:** ~300 | **Difficulty:** HARD

**Mechanics:**
- **Forces you to choose between 2 escalating debuffs** every cycle
- Heals + gains Str on turn 4
- Debuffs STACK - Disintegration damage adds up!

**Attack Pattern:**
1. Strategic (Choose a debuff)
2. Attack
3. Attack x3
4. Attack + Heal + Empower (+Str)
5. *Repeats*

**Debuff Choices:**
| Choice | Option A | Option B |
|--------|----------|----------|
| 1st | Disintegration I (6 dmg/turn) | Mind Rot (draw 1 fewer) |
| 2nd | Disintegration II (+7 dmg/turn) | Sloth (max 3 cards/turn) |
| 3rd | Disintegration III (+8 dmg/turn) | Waste Away (-1 energy/turn) |

**Strategy:**
- **KILL BEFORE 3RD CHOICE**
- Take Mind Rot first if you can't play whole hand anyway
- Then take Disintegration II (7 dmg < Sloth restriction)
- High block can take all Disintegration

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Fast infinite = kill before choices matter
- ✅ High damage prevents stacking debuffs
- ⚠️ Sloth (3 cards/turn) BREAKS infinite - avoid!
- ⚠️ Waste Away cripples energy-hungry decks

### Kaiser Crab 🦀
**HP:** ~400 (split between 2 claws) | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Mechanics:**
- **Two claws attack separately** - Crusher (left) and Rocket (right)
- **Direction matters** - face toward biggest attack for less damage
- Killing one claw buffs the other (+Block, +Str)

**Attack Pattern:**
| Turn | Crusher (Left) | Rocket (Right) |
|------|----------------|----------------|
| 1 | Big Attack | Attack |
| 2 | Attack | Big Attack |
| 3 | Attack x2 + Weak/Frail | Empower (+Str) |
| 4 | Empower (+Str) | MASSIVE Attack |
| 5 | Attack + Block | Reload |

**Strategy:**
- Face toward the biggest attack each turn!
- Attack a claw before ending turn to face it
- Rocket has bigger burst, Crusher has consistent damage
- Kill one claw, then handle the buffed survivor

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Single-target focus one claw down
- ✅ AoE damages both, but doesn't help facing
- ⚠️ Turn 4 Rocket MASSIVE attack - block hard!

# ACT 3 BOSSES (Glory)

### Queen & Torch Head Amalgam 👑
**HP:** ~300 (Queen) + minion | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Mechanics:**
- **Chains of Binding** - 3 random cards affected, can only play 1 of them
- Queen buffs minion + blocks herself
- Kill minion first OR burn Queen and tank minion damage

**Queen Pattern:**
1. Chains of Binding (3 cards restricted)
2. Strategic (99 Frail, Weak, Vulnerable!)
3. Empower minion + Block self
4. *Continues buffing/blocking*

**Minion Pattern:**
1. Attack
2. Attack
3. Attack x3
4. *Repeats*

**After Minion Dies - Queen Pattern:**
1. Empower (+Str to self)
2. Attack x5
3. Attack
4. *Repeats*

**Strategy:**
- Chains of Binding can be played around (Hellraiser, etc.)
- Turn 2 debuff is brutal - block or kill fast
- Kill minion → then Queen before she buffs too much

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Exhaust helps with Chains (fewer cards to chain)
- ✅ AoE damages both
- ⚠️ Turn 2 = 99 Frail/Weak/Vulnerable! Block or burst

### Test Subject C#18 🧪
**HP:** ~450 total (3 phases) | **Difficulty:** Hard

**Mechanics:**
- **3 PHASES** - boss revives twice with new abilities
- Phase 1: Enrage (+Str when you play Skills)
- Phase 2: Inflicts Wounds on unblocked damage
- Phase 3: Intangible every other turn

**Phase 1 Pattern:**
1. Attack
2. Attack + Vulnerability
3. *Repeats*

**Phase 2 Pattern:**
1. Attack x3
2. Attack
3. Attack x4
4. *Repeats*

**Phase 3 Pattern:**
1. Attack x3
2. Attack
3. Burns + Empower (+Str)
4. Attack x3
5. *Repeats*

**Strategy:**
- Phase 1: Use Attacks, minimize Skills (Enrage)
- Phase 2: Block well! Wounds persist to Phase 3
- Phase 3: Multi-hit attacks counter Intangible (each hit = 1 dmg)

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Phase 1 favors attack-heavy decks (Ironclad!)
- ✅ Exhaust clears Wounds from Phase 2
- ✅ Multi-hit (Sword Boomerang, Twin Strike) for Phase 3
- ⚠️ Setup turns on Intangible, then burst

---

### Door / Doormaker 🚪
**HP:** ~155 (Door) + ~300 (Doormaker) | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Mechanics:**
- **Two-phase repeating fight**
- Kill Door → Doormaker appears (stunned turn 1)
- Doormaker attacks for 3 turns → transforms back to Door
- Door regens full HP, Doormaker does NOT heal

**Door Pattern:**
1. Attack
2. Attack x2 (15 each)
3. Attack + Empower (+Str)
4. *Repeats*

**Doormaker Pattern:**
1. Stunned (FREE DAMAGE!)
2. Attack
3. BIG Attack + Empower + Escape (→ Door)

**Strategy:**
- Burst the Door, damage carries over on Doormaker
- Save block for Doormaker turn 3 big attack
- Doormaker doesn't heal - whittle him down over cycles

**Ironclad Tips:**
- ✅ Stun turn = dump all damage
- ✅ Save block cards for Doormaker turn 3
- ⚠️ Don't kill Door without block in hand for Doormaker

# BOSS PRIORITY BY ARCHETYPE

## Exhaust Infinite
- ✅ Vantom (exhaust Wounds)
- ✅ Soul Fysh (exhaust Beckon)
- ⚠️ Lagavulin Matriarch (setup time helps, but debuffs hurt)
- ⚠️ Knowledge Demon (Sloth breaks infinite!)

## Vulnerable Synergy (Vicious)
- ✅ Lagavulin Matriarch (setup turns to play Vicious)
- ✅ Test Subject Phase 3 (Intangible - multi-hit with vuln)
- ⚠️ Queen (99 Vulnerable on YOU turn 2)

## High Burst Damage
- ✅ Knowledge Demon (kill before debuffs stack)
- ✅ Waterfall Giant (minimize explosion)
- ✅ Doormaker (stun turns = free damage)
