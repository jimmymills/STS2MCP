## Silent Run Notes

### Run 1: Sly/Shiv Hybrid - DIED to Act 1 Boss (Lagavulin Matriarch)

**What went wrong:**
- Deck too bloated: **20 cards** at boss
- Only 1 Strike removed (should have been 3-4)
- Mixed Sly AND Shiv without committing to either
- Took Blade Dance without Knife Trap or Accuracy
- Found Acrobatics too late (right before boss)

**Key learnings:**
1. **Remove aggressively** — Target 12-15 cards by Act 1 boss, not 20
2. **Don't take Blade Dance without payoffs** — Knife Trap or Accuracy FIRST
3. **Engine before payoffs** — Acrobatics/Tools of the Trade > more Sly cards
4. **Commit to ONE archetype** — Sly OR Shiv, not both (unless Knife Trap bridges them)
5. **Lagavulin Matriarch** is hard — -Str/-Dex stacks kill you. Need fast damage.

### Run 2: Lean Sly - DIED to Act 1 Elite

**What went wrong:**
- **Injury curse** from Potion Belt event — dead draw clogging deck
- Low HP (36-40/70) going into elite
- Only 1 Sly payoff (Flick-Flack) — not enough damage output
- Didn't prioritize curse removal at shop

**What went right:**
- Found Acrobatics on **floor 2** (excellent!)
- Stayed lean (15 cards including curse)
- Good skip discipline
- Upgraded Acrobatics immediately
- Took second Acrobatics

**Key learnings:**
1. **Avoid curse-giving events** unless you can immediately remove
2. **Don't fight elites below 50% HP** without potions ready
3. **Path around elites** if HP is low — skipping elites is okay
4. **Need multiple Sly payoffs** — Acrobatics is engine, need Reflex/Untouchable/Flick-Flack for damage
5. **Curse removal is TOP PRIORITY** — prioritize over Strike removal if you have a curse

## General Lessons (All Characters)

### Deck Size
- **Act 1 target:** 12-15 cards by boss
- **Act 2 target:** 10-14 cards (smaller = better for loops)
- **Act 3 target:** 8-12 cards for infinite potential
- Every card you add should EARN its slot

### When to Skip Cards
- No synergy with current build
- Archetype not yet committed (wait for key pieces)
- Already have enough of that effect
- Would bloat deck past target size

### When to Take Cards
- S-tier cards (Acrobatics, etc.) — always
- Key archetype pieces (Knife Trap for Shivs, Accelerant for Poison)
- Fills a critical gap (no block, no damage, no draw)
- Exhausts (leaves deck, keeps it small)

### Elite Fighting Rules
- **Above 70% HP:** Fight freely
- **50-70% HP:** Fight if deck is good, have potions
- **Below 50% HP:** AVOID unless path forces it
- **Below 30% HP:** Absolutely avoid — path to rest site

### Curse Management
- Remove curses BEFORE Strikes at shop
- Avoid curse events unless removal is guaranteed
- Curses are worse than they look (dead draws kill runs)

### Rest vs Upgrade Decisions
- **Below 50% HP:** Usually rest
- **50-70% HP:** Upgrade if good target exists
- **Above 70% HP:** Almost always upgrade
- **Key upgrades (Acrobatics+, etc.):** Upgrade even at 50%

## Archetype-Specific Notes

### Silent: Sly/Discard
- **Acrobatics** is the #1 priority — take every copy
- **Tools of the Trade** enables auto-Sly every turn
- Need 2-3 Sly payoffs (Reflex, Flick-Flack, Untouchable)
- Well-Laid Plans lines up perfect discard turns

### Silent: Shivs
- **Knife Trap > Accuracy** — Knife Trap alone wins runs
- Don't take Blade Dance without Knife Trap or Accuracy
- After Image is #1 support card (block per card played)
- Shivs exhausting feeds Knife Trap

### Silent: Poison
- **Accelerant is the key** — hard pivot if you see it
- Poison ticks 2-3x with Accelerant = massive damage
- Noxious Fumes + Bubble Bubble + Accelerant = boss killer
- Don't take poison cards without Accelerant

## Boss-Specific Notes

### Soul Fysh
- Adds Beckon cards (lose HP if not played)
- Play/exhaust Beckons immediately
- Gets Intangible every 4th turn — don't dump damage
- Kill fast before deck floods

### Lagavulin Matriarch
- Sleeps 3 turns — USE FOR SETUP
- -Str/-Dex debuffs stack and cripple you
- Kill FAST or debuffs make you deal 0 damage
- Piercing Wail helps vs multi-hit

### Knowledge Demon
- Forces debuff choices every few turns
- Sloth (3 card limit) breaks infinite loops!
- Take Mind Rot (draw -1) first if doing Sly
- Kill before 3rd debuff choice

## Card Evaluation Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Taking payoffs without enablers** — Accuracy without Shivs, Poison cards without Accelerant
2. **Taking "win more" cards early** — Master Planner is great when winning, doesn't help you get there
3. **Ignoring exhaust value** — Cards that exhaust keep deck small
4. **Overvaluing block** — Killing fast often better than blocking forever
5. **Taking second copies of exhaust cards** — One Piercing Wail is enough
