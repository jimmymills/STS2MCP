# Slay the Spire 2 - Elite Enemies Reference

> Elite enemies drop relics when defeated and are key to scaling power during a run. Most Act 1 runs should aim for 1-2 elites if HP allows.

---

## Act 1a: Overgrowth Elites

### Bygone Effigy
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 127 (Asc: 132) |
| Threat | Medium |
| Starting Power | Slow 1 |

**Mechanics:**
- Starts **asleep** — does nothing on Turn 1
- Has **Slow** effect: takes increasing damage from each card played per turn
- Wakes up Turn 2 and gains **10 Strength**
- Then deals 15 (Asc: 17) damage every turn

**Move Pattern:**
1. Initial Sleep (nothing)
2. Wake (gains Str 10)
3. Slashes (15 damage) — repeats

**Strategy:**
- ✅ Use Turn 1 to set up powers or draw cards
- ✅ Play many attacks in one turn to maximize Slow damage
- ✅ Multi-attack cards excel here
- ❌ Don't spread weak attacks across multiple turns

**Best Cards:** Whirlwind, Blade Dance, multi-hit attacks, setup powers

### Byrdonis
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 91-94 (Asc: 99) |
| Threat | **HIGH** |
| Starting Power | Territorial 1 |

**Mechanics:**
- Alternates between **Peck** (3×3 damage) and **SWOOP** (16 damage)
- Gains **Strength every turn** — fights get deadly fast
- Very aggressive, punishes slow decks

**Move Pattern:**
1. SWOOP (16 damage)
2. Peck (3×3 damage)
— repeats

**Strategy:**
- ✅ **Kill quickly** — Strength stacking is lethal
- ✅ Apply **Weak** to reduce damage
- ✅ Burst damage is key
- ❌ Don't let the fight drag on

**Best Cards:** High burst damage, Vulnerable application, Weak

---

### Phrog Parasite
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 61-64 (Asc: 66-68) |
| Threat | Medium-High |
| Starting Power | Infested 4 |

**Mechanics:**
- Adds **Infection** status cards to your discard pile
- Alternates between **Infect** (adds 3 Infections) and **Lash** (4×4 damage)
- On death, spawns **Wrigglers** — fight continues!

**Move Pattern:**
1. Infect (3 Infections added)
2. Lash (4×4 damage)
— repeats

**Wrigglers (spawned):**
- HP: 17-21 (Asc: 18-22)
- Uses Nasty Bite (6 damage), Wriggle (gains Str +2, adds Infection)
- Low HP but Strength gain makes them dangerous if ignored

**Strategy:**
- ✅ Save energy/cards for the second phase (Wrigglers)
- ✅ AoE damage helps clear spawns
- ✅ Exhaust to manage status cards
- ❌ Don't exhaust all resources on the main enemy

**Best Cards:** AoE attacks, exhaust effects, card draw

## Act 1b: Underdocks Elites

### Phantasmal Gardener
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 28-32 (Asc: 29-33) |
| Threat | Medium |
| Starting Power | Gains Block on first hit each turn |

**Mechanics:**
- Gains **Block the first time it takes damage each turn**
- Punishes multi-hit attacks (Shivs, Claw, etc.)
- Rewards strong single hits
- Multiple enemies appear in this encounter

**Move Pattern:**
Position-dependent start, then cycles: Flail → Enlarge → Bite → Lash

**Strategy:**
- ✅ Use strong single attacks first
- ✅ Trigger the Block, then hit hard
- ❌ Don't waste multiple small attacks early in turn
- ❌ Avoid multi-hit strategies

**Best Cards:** High single-hit damage, Vulnerable application

### Skulking Colony
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 79 (Asc: 84) |
| Threat | Medium-High |
| Starting Power | **Hardened Shell 20** |

**Mechanics:**
- **Cannot lose more than 20 HP per turn** (Hardened Shell)
- Adds **Dazed** status cards to your discard
- Fight will **always last multiple turns** — burst damage ineffective
- Gains Strength and Block

**Moves:**
- **Inertia:** Gains Str 3, gains 10 Block
- **Zoom:** Deals 16 damage
- **Super Crab:** Deals 6×2 damage
- **SMASH:** Deals 9 damage, adds 4 Dazed

**Strategy:**
- ✅ Focus on **consistent damage** over multiple turns
- ✅ Prepare for a long fight — bring defense
- ✅ Use card draw to manage Dazed status clutter
- ❌ Don't try to burst — damage cap prevents it

**Best Cards:** Consistent damage, defense, card draw

---

### Terror Eel
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 140 (Asc: 150) |
| Threat | **HIGH** |
| Starting Power | **Shriek 70** (stun threshold) |

**Mechanics:**
- Deals heavy damage with attacks
- **CRASH:** 17 damage | **Thrash:** 3×3 damage + gains Vigor 7
- When HP drops below **70** for the first time → becomes **Stunned** (loses next action)
- After stun: uses **TERROR** (applies 99 Vulnerable!) then resumes attacking

**Move Pattern:**
- Phase 1: CRASH → Thrash (repeats)
- At HP < 70: STUN (skips turn)
- Phase 2: TERROR → CRASH → Thrash (repeats)

**Strategy:**
- ✅ Push it below stun threshold at the right moment
- ✅ Use the stun turn to deal massive damage
- ✅ Block well before triggering stun
- ❌ Don't trigger stun too early — waste the free turn
- ⚠️ After stun, you take **50% more damage** from TERROR's Vulnerable

**Best Cards:** High burst damage, strong defense, timing-aware plays

## Act 2: Hive Elites

### Decimillipede
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 42-48 per segment (Asc: 48-56) |
| Threat | Medium-High |
| Starting Power | Multi-segment |

**Mechanics:**
- Multiple segments that attack together
- Defeated segments can **revive** if other segments are still alive
- Requires sustained focused damage on one target

**Strategy:**
- ✅ **Focus down one segment at a time**
- ✅ Sustained single-target damage wins
- ❌ AoE damage is less effective here

**Best Cards:** Single-target damage, consistent output

### Entomancer
| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 145 (Asc: 155) |
| Threat | **HIGH** |
| Starting Power | Personal Hive 1 |

**Mechanics:**
- Adds **Dazed cards to your draw pile** whenever hit by an Attack
- Can quickly clog your hand with unplayable cards
- Gains Strength over time

**Moves:**
- **Beeeees!:** Deals 3×7 damage
- **Spear!:** Deals 18 damage
- **Pheromone Spit:** Gains Personal Hive +1, gains Strength

**Move Pattern:** Beeeees! → Spear! → Pheromone Spit (repeats)

**Strategy:**
- ✅ **Minimize Attack card plays** — use Skills for damage when possible
- ✅ Exhaust Dazed cards
- ✅ Prioritize exhaust synergies
- ❌ Don't spam attacks — you'll clog your deck

**Best Cards:** Skill-based damage, exhaust effects, card draw

## Act 3: Glory Elites

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Act 3 elites are boss-level threats. Only fight with a fully assembled deck.

### Knight Trio (Flail Knight + Spectral Knight + Magi Knight)
| Knight | HP | Asc HP |
|--------|-----|--------|
| Flail Knight | 101 | 108 |
| Spectral Knight | 93 | 97 |
| Magi Knight | 82 | 89 |

**Mechanics:**
- **Three enemies at once** with different attack styles
- Flail Knight: Physical damage, War Chant (Str +3), Flail (9×2), Ram (15)
- Spectral Knight: Spectral attacks that may bypass Block
- Magi Knight: Magical attacks with debuffs (lowest HP)

**Move Pattern (Flail Knight):**
- Starts with Ram
- Randomly uses War Chant, Flail, or Ram (no same move twice in a row for War Chant)

**Strategy:**
- ✅ **Kill Magi Knight first** — lowest HP, removes debuffs
- ✅ Requires strong **AoE damage**
- ✅ Prepare for a long multi-enemy fight
- ❌ Don't ignore any knight — all deal significant damage

**Best Cards:** AoE damage, multi-target attacks, strong defense

## General Elite Fighting Tips

### Before the Fight
- Check your HP — can you afford damage?
- Review your deck — do you have counters?
- Save potions for elites, not normal fights
- **You can't know which elite until you enter the room** — prepare for the worst case
- Consider skipping if unprepared

### During the Fight
- Use potions early — don't save for "later"
- Play around their mechanics
- Prioritize killing fast over perfect defense
- Watch attack patterns and plan ahead

### Fight Elites When:
- ✅ Deck has improved after a few fights
- ✅ You have 50%+ HP remaining
- ✅ You have potions to use
- ✅ Your deck has counters for that elite
- ✅ It's Act 1 and you need relics to scale

### Skip Elites When:
- ❌ Very low HP (below 30%)
- ❌ Deck hasn't improved yet
- ❌ You lack specific counters
- ❌ It's Act 3 and you're unprepared
- ❌ Better to reach boss alive than die to elite
